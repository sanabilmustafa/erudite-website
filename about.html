About Us

WHAT IS ERUDITE? Why we named it ERUDITE?

learn more

Erudite is the brainchild of Founder Muhammad Ali. It was a desire to establish a professional workplace that would be recognized for its diversified professional services including but not limited to Accounting, Finance, Business consultancy, Taxation, Data Analysis, Business Development & Analysis etc. Not only that, Erudite is established with an aim to provide all our clients with professional resources where and when required / desired.

The name is not our identity; rather, it is an expression of identifying our key competences as Erudite, which was created by a single mind but has been gladly embraced by a family of Accounting, Finance & Business professionals who are co-members of the business and whose knowledge will set the bar high in several disciplines.
